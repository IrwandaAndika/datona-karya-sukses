HR Management Consulting

Businesses sometimes can't financially justify the costs of handling benefits, insurance, payroll, taxes, workers' compensation, constantly varying regulations and other overwhelming human resource (HR) tasks. As a result HR is generally overlooked and often regarded as unimportant, or at best a grudge purchase. Like many other businesses, you can still operate without having to spend your valuable time attending to HR issues like employment contracts, job descriptions, skills development, policies and procedures, etc. Until you have an employee problem and you need to take action, not having the basics in place can cost you time and money. We understand and provide customized consultancy in all aspects of your HR. In collaboration with the best brains in the business of Human Resource, we bring you simply the best solution to your Human Resource needs. We listen, we understand and provide you with the best solution customized for your business.

Industrial Relation Consulting

Industrial relations is increasingly being called employment relations or employee relations because of the importance of non-industrial employment relationships. Human resource management is now synonymous with employee relations. Industrial relations studies examine various employment situations, not just ones with a unionized workforce. At Datona, we understand the challenges of managing a business. You need to keep focused on what you do, and readily adapt to the needs of your customers to keep competitive and retain market share. All change, whether it be through growth, restructuring, products, mergers, downsizing or financially, impacts on employees in some way. Datona is all about providing you with the support you need – from business start up, through growth and change or adversity. We will help you lay down the foundations to enable good people practices.
