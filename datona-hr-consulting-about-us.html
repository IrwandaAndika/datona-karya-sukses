Datona Consulting is a HR/IR Consulting Company. We understand and provide the best consultancy in all aspects of your Human Resources Management and Industrial Relationship. We also have experience in sourcing specialised skills. We provide tailor made services according to our clients’ specifications and company culture.

Our consultants are all highly qualified HR Professionals with strong HR expertise in the Asia Pacific region and Indonesia in particular. We are dedicated to providing your business with a competitive edge by providing pro-active best practice solutions and advice, tailored to meet your industrial relations and human resources needs. Our core competence is also Executive Search, Talent Sourcing and we have the strategy and global reach to locate, screen, evaluate and select top talents for our clients.

  • Wide Industrial Network

Our network of consultants range across Indonesia and Southeast Asia region with vast industrial expertise and knowledge in many areas of industries. Our Clients range from top Multinationals in the Fortune 500 list to local medium sized companies.

Datona Consulting has over the years clearly established its reputation as a premier HR Consulting firm with a string of success stories in the form of positions closed for middle to senior level executives positions. Relevant sectors include: Manufacturing, FMCG, Oil & Gas, Banking, Services, Finance, and Human Resources.
